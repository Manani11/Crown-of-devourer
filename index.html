Crown of the Devourer

By Drunky

🔥 Synopsis

Born a mistake. Cast into hell. Raised by demons. Now... he returns to devour the world that cast him out.

Amon was thrown away at birth, branded as a failure by the royal bloodline. Raised in the abyss, where gods fear to look, he clawed his way through death and betrayal. Twenty years later, the child they abandoned returns—not as a man, but as the Devourer of Kings, heir to a power older than time itself.

"You think I care for trust? Trust is just another tool. And I sharpen my tools well."
